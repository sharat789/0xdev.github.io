How I Got Here

I got into the micro-blogging space 2 years ago when I first started my job as a Frontend Platform Engineer. It all began when I noticed my colleague casually browsing Hacker News while sipping his morning coffee—which quickly became my ritual too.

I was impressed by how everyday developers had their own blog websites where they shared knowledge and experience with the world, often with a tinge of humor and sarcasm that made technical content a very nice addition to your daily monotonous task of asking people to rebase their PR when shit doesn’t work.
